About me

Hello! My name is Naga Gayatri Bandaru, a dedicated and proficient software engineer specializing in computer software and engineering. I am currently pursuing my Master of Science in Computer Software and Engineering at San José State University, having previously earned my Bachelor of Technology in Computer Science and Engineering from Koneru Lakshmaiah University with a CGPA of 8.08.

My passion for technology is demonstrated through various personal projects, including the development of a Facebook-integrated serverless chatbot using Amazon Lex, a serverless employee information retrieval web application on AWS Lambda, and a gesture-based interaction system showcasing advanced hand gesture recognition. I have also applied machine learning techniques for COVID-19 spread forecasting, utilizing Support Vector Machines and Reinforcement Learning.

I have hands-on experience in architecting, deploying, and administering AWS solutions, acquired during my AWS Cloud Virtual Internship at AICTE-Eduskills. My technical proficiency extends to programming languages such as C/C++, Python, and JavaScript, and I am well-versed in libraries like ReactJs and various Python libraries. My interests are primarily focused on web design and development, as well as cloud security, and I hold an AWS Certified Solutions Architect Associate certification, demonstrating my expertise in architecting efficient solutions on AWS.

In addition to my technical skills, I pride myself on my problem-solving abilities, adaptability, self-learning capabilities, and presentation skills. My research, including work on interactive projection technology using hand gesture recognition with attention mechanism and ResNet, has been published in IEEE Xplore, reflecting my commitment to contributing to the field of computer science and engineering.
